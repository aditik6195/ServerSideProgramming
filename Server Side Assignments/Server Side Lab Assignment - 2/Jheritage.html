Heritage of Jaipur

Explore the rich cultural and historical heritage of the Pink City

Amber Fort

Amber Fort, located in Amer, is a majestic fort built with red sandstone and marble. It is known for its artistic Hindu-style elements and offers breathtaking views of Maota Lake.

Hawa Mahal

The Hawa Mahal, or "Palace of Winds," is a stunning five-story structure with 953 small windows (jharokhas) decorated with intricate latticework. It was built to allow royal ladies to observe street festivals without being seen.

City Palace

The City Palace is a magnificent complex that houses museums, courtyards, and gardens. It showcases a blend of Mughal and Rajasthani architecture and remains a residence for the royal family.

Jantar Mantar

Jantar Mantar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and an astronomical observatory built by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II. It features a collection of 19 architectural astronomical instruments.
